Murgh Kari: Chicken Curry


Murgh Kari is an Indian Curry that combines the spices garam masala, coriander, tumeric, and cayenne pepper with a blend of tomato, yogurt, and onion to create a smooth sauce to poor over rice or simply eat with fresh naan.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless, bonelss chicken breasts
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons minced ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Cooking Instructions

  1. Salt and Brown Chicken Breast - Put Aside
  2. On Medium heat, add: Onion, Garlic, and Ginger to some oil in the pan
    • Stir mixture until soft and translucent
    • Then stir in: Curry Powder, Cumin, Tumeric, Coriander, Cayenne, and 1 Tbs of water
    • Stir for about 1 minute
  3. Add tomatoes, yogurt, 1 Tbs chopped cilantro, and 1 tsp salt. Stir to combine
  4. Reincorporate chicken and its juices
    • Add 1/2 cup water and bring to boil
    • Turn chicken to coat in sauce
    • Sprinkle Garam Masala and 1 Tbsp cilantro over chicken
  5. Cover Pan with Lid and Simmer until Chicken cooked
    • Drizzle Lemon Juice to Serve
